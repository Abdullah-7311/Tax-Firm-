01 — Global Tax

Decoding the Shifting Global Tax Map

Regulatory Intelligence

The global tax landscape is undergoing its most significant structural evolution in a generation. Driven by the OECD's landmark Pillar Two framework, country-level tax agencies across more than 140 jurisdictions are simultaneously closing historic loopholes, enforcing global minimum corporate tax rates of 15%, and fundamentally rewriting the rules of cross-border profit allocation.

03 — Macro Risk

Inflation, Interest Rates & Cash Liquidity

Economic Risk

Persistent economic inflation distorts historical cost accounting, erodes real operating profit margins, inflates reported asset values, and dramatically drives up the cost of debt capital — simultaneously pressuring working capital cycles and tightening liquidity across balance sheets that were structured for a lower-rate environment.

We advise clients on optimal inventory valuation methods — navigating between FIFO and weighted average cost methodologies during inflationary stretches to ensure balance sheet reporting accurately reflects realistic replacement costs without artificially inflating taxable income. Simultaneously, we help leadership teams systematically tighten accounts receivable collection cycles and negotiate extended accounts payable terms, reducing dependency on expensive revolving credit lines.

Working Capital Strategy

For mid-market companies, a 10-day improvement in Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) combined with a 7-day extension of Days Payable Outstanding (DPO) typically frees 3–6% of annual revenue in trapped working capital — without any external financing required.
